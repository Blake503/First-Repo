Otters

A little bit About The River Otter

Most people think that otters are just cute and cuddly animals but, I can assure you that they are much more than that. Otters can grow up to 3 to 4 feet, and can get up to 30lbs in weight. They often live in dens built by other river animals like beavers.

A River Otters Eating Habits

River otters eat many things such as birds, crabs, fish, and even turtles. When most people think of otters they think of a cute animal, but when an otter is eating it is not pretty. They look almost like they have not eaten in weeks and most of the food they eat does not really end up in their mouth. When the otter eats it almost looks like the devil

Places you can find the river otter

  1. Alaska
  2. Canada
  3. Gulf Of Mexico
  4. California
Features

Otters have Brown, grey and, yellow fur. They also have long and skinny bodies, they have very poweerful webbed feet. They have very sharp claws and can breath under water for a long time.
